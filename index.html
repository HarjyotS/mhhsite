MSET Health Hackathon

Jan 28 - Jan 29 2023

What this this hackathon?

The MSET health hackathon is an event we plan to run to help the earth. The hackathon centers around the idea of creating a product that is related to health using programming. The hackathon is an in person focused event with the first day online and the second day in person with lots of fun activities.

What are the rules?

  • Ages: 13-18
  • Teams: 1-3 people
  • Version Control: Must be used
